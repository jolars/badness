\section{Introduction}

This is a small, self-contained \LaTeX{} document used as the zero-network
baseline for the formatter benchmark. It mixes a handful of common constructs so
the benchmark exercises sectioning, inline and display math, lists, tables, and
environments rather than one narrow shape.

Inline math such as $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$ sits next to prose, and a display follows:
\begin{equation}
    \int_0^1 x^2 \, \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{3}.
\end{equation}
